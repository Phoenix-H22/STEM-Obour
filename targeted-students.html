Targeted Students

The Eligibilty Criteria Changed by STEM Unit Every Year.




We Accept only about 150 students annually.



Undoubtedly, the school targets proposed students with demonstrated passion and aptitude for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.



Students coming to STEM Obour should show their enthusiasm to continuously identify, improve themselves, and share their awareness with the school community.

ELIGIBILTY CRITERIA

In order to get accepted into STEM October, applicants should meet one of two criteria:

  • To have a total grade of more than 98% in the 9th Grade, and to have a grade of 100% in one of three Subjects: Maths, Science, and English.
  • To have a total grade of more than 95% in the 9th Grade, and to have a grade of 100% in two of three Subjects: Maths, Science, and English.

These criteria vary each year; however, they have been the same for the last two years and are expected to last for a couple more.
After achieving one of the mentioned conditions, applicants encounter four other acceptance tests - which are made to assure the applicants’ capability in the tested fields. The four tests are English, Maths, Science, and IQ and are held nation-wide. Students are Eventually distributed to schools according to their grades.
